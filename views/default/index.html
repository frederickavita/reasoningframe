Frequently Asked Questions & Objections

Total transparency on our rates and conditions.

Monthly Subscription (€29)

What does "No commitment" actually mean?

It means zero constraints. You can unsubscribe in 1 click from your dashboard. Access stops at the end of the paid month. Ideal for testing reliability on 1 or 2 accounts.

Why am I limited to 2 accounts?

This offer is an entry point. We want to encourage the Lifetime offer (5 accounts) which is mathematically more profitable for you starting from the 5th month.

Freelance License (€149)

Do I have to pay for future updates?

NON. It's €0/year.
That's the principle of the "Lifetime" deal. Your €149 payment covers software access AND future Google API compatibility updates. No hidden fees, no renewal.

Unlimited Budget: Really? Even for €100k spend?

Absolutely. Unlike Optmyzr or Opteo, we don't apply a "growth tax". Whether you secure €500 or €500,000, the server load is identical for us. So the price remains €149, full stop.

How does the "Money-Back" guarantee work?

Simple: If within 14 days, you find that the tool doesn't bring the peace of mind promised, send an email. We refund your €149 in full, no questions asked.

Agency Setup (€3,500)

What guarantees longevity after paying €3,500?

The beauty of Self-Hosting. You pay for the infrastructure installation on YOUR servers. You own the code. Even if ReasoningFrame disappears tomorrow, your system continues to run eternally because you hold the keys.

How much is maintenance after the 1st year?

The first year is included. Then, it's optional: you can subscribe to a maintenance contract for €400 / year. This covers: security updates, Google Ads API compatibility (which evolves often), and the priority SLA (< 4h).

What exactly is the < 4h SLA?

As an Agency, if you have a critical blocker (e.g., unable to connect a new large client), our engineering team commits to intervening within 4 business hours. This is an "Enterprise" service level essential for your critical operations.
