Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to know how to code?

No. ReasoningFrame is designed to work with tools like Cursor, Lovable, or Bolt.new. We generate the instructions (the "Prompt") that you feed to the AI. The AI does the coding.

How does the Reddit analysis work?

We use semantic algorithms to filter out the noise. We specifically look for negative sentiments ("pain points") and unmet feature requests within targeted communities.

Can I use the results commercially?

Yes. Everything ReasoningFrame generates (Personas, Specs, Ideas) belongs 100% to you. You can sell the reports or build the SaaS.
