What is reasoningframe?

It's an AI-powered platform that generates instant programming micro-courses. Instead of watching someone else code, you get a personalized lesson tailored to your specific goal (Python, JS, HTML, or CSS) so you can start building immediately.

How does it work?

Type an intention like "I want to learn CSS Flexbox for app layouts." Our engine generates a structured guide including the core logic, real-world code examples, and an interactive challenge. No fluff, just the code.

Why is it better than video courses?

Videos are passive. Research shows you learn faster by doing. reasoningframe eliminates the "scroll-and-search" struggle by giving you exactly the explanation and snippet you need for your current project.

How do credits work?

1 Credit = 1 Micro-course generated. We don't believe in monthly subscriptions that you forget to use. Buy credits, use them whenever you face a coding roadblock, and they never expire.

Can I access my courses later?

Yes! Every course you generate is saved to your personal library. You can revisit your snippets, explanations, and challenges anytime by signing in with your Google account.

Which languages are supported?

Currently, we specialize in the Full-Stack core: Python, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. We focus on these to ensure the highest pedagogical quality and most accurate code generation.
